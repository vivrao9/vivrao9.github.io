About me

I'm a graduate student at Purdue University studying business analytics and information management. I recently completed a bachelor's in finance and business analytics.

Most recently, I was a Data and Analytics intern at Eli Lilly where I used the Microsoft Power Platform to set up apps that streamline the data collection process within the clinical trials ecosystem. One of my apps was later used for the COVID-19 antibody trials.

I served as managing editor for digital at the Indiana Daily Student between January 2020 and May 2021, overseeing web traffic grow more than 30% between 2019 and 2020. I also designed pages for the print newspaper, sending them off to the presses on strict deadline. I was one of the founding members of the Arnolt Center for Investigative Journalism.

I love puzzles and do the New York Times' sudoku (almost) every day. I last did it on 10/25/2021 and finished it in 3:01.
