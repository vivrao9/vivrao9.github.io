About me

I create data visualizations, dashboards, websites, and digital tools that communicate complex ideas in simple ways.

I'm a huge fan of cross-functional collaboration between designers and developers. I'm comfortable using Figma, d3.js, React, Tableau, PowerBI, Python, SQL, the Adobe Creative Suite, and more.
